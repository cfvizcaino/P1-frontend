Interactive Cosmic Time Machine: Visualizing Light Travel Across the Universe

A web-based application that allows users to explore how light travels through space and how observing distant celestial objects means looking back in time. Users can input or select astronomical objects (like the Moon, planets, stars, or galaxies) to see how long their light takes to reach Earth. The tool provides a dynamic visualization showing the concept of light-years, the scale of the universe, and real-time comparisons
